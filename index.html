Who I Am

More Than Just Code

A driven builder who believes technology is a tool for meaningful impact — not just a skill set.

I'm Gilbert Maghanga Nyange, a fourth-year BBIT student at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology, with a deep passion for software development, systems design, and building things that actually matter.

From a young age, technology fascinated me as a medium for solving problems. That fascination evolved into full-stack expertise — from crafting seamless UIs with React to architecting secure REST APIs with Node.js and JWT, backed by MongoDB and MySQL databases.

"To use technology as a tool for creating impactful, meaningful, and efficient solutions that solve real-world problems and empower organizations."

Beyond the screen, I'm the Deputy Chess Captain of DeKUT's Chess Club — a role that sharpened my strategic thinking, leadership, and ability to stay composed under pressure. When I'm not coding or playing chess, you'll find me admiring the view of Mount Kenya, exploring philosophy and personal development, or reflecting on faith and purpose.
